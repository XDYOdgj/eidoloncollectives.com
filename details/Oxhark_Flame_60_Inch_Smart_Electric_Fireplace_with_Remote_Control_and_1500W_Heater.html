Oxhark Flame 60 Inch Smart Electric Fireplace

This electric fireplace is the perfect combination of modern design and smart home convenience. Whether you're looking to add a touch of warmth to your living room or create a cozy atmosphere in your bedroom, the Oxhark Flame 60 Inch unit delivers both style and function without the complexities of a traditional fireplace.

Designed with a sleek, slim profile and featuring a metal frame with tempered glass, the Oxhark Flame 60 Inch electric fireplace beautifully blends into any room. It is available in both wall-mounted and recessed configurations, giving you the freedom to choose the ideal fit for your space. From living rooms to offices, this versatile appliance can become a focal point in any setting.

Equipped with a powerful 1500W heater, it efficiently warms up to 400 square feet, keeping your space cozy and comfortable. The built-in thermostat ensures a safe and consistent temperature range between 62°F and 99°F, while the timer function lets you set it for up to 9 hours, making it perfect for relaxation or energy efficiency.

Customize Your Atmosphere

Enhance your living space with the option to choose from 13 flame colors and 13 fuel bed colors, allowing you to create the perfect visual ambiance that matches your mood or interior design. Whether for a dramatic fire scene or a subtle glow, the customizable flame and fuel bed settings offer endless possibilities.

Smart Control at Your Fingertips

Enjoy the convenience of intuitive smart control with Wi-Fi-enabled features, allowing you to manage the fireplace via touch screen, remote, or through a mobile app. This seamless integration makes it effortless to adjust your heating and ambiance settings from anywhere in your home.

Safe and Efficient Heating

With overheat protection and a dust-free, smog-free operation, this fireplace is designed to keep your family safe, especially in homes with pets and children. The tempered glass and modern design add a touch of elegance while ensuring long-lasting performance and ease of use.
