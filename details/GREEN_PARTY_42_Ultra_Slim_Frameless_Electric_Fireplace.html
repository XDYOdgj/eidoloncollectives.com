Elevate Your Living Room with the GREEN PARTY 42" Electric Fireplace

Experience the warmth and beauty of flames without the hassle of gas, wood, or chimney maintenance. This electric fireplace combines sleek modern style with efficient heating, making it the perfect addition to any home.

The GREEN PARTY 42" Electric Fireplace features an ultra-slim, frameless design with a 1" bezel that enhances the visual impact of its lifelike LED flames. Whether you're looking to create a cozy atmosphere for a quiet evening or set the mood for a lively gathering, this fireplace adapts to your needs seamlessly.

With realistic LED flames that come in 7 colors and can be adjusted for brightness and speed, you're in control of the ambiance. The dual heat settings—750W low and 1500W high—along with a flame-only option, provide flexibility for both comfort and aesthetics.

Installation is easy with wall mounting or recessed options, and included hardware ensures you can place it wherever you like. Customize your experience using the built-in remote or touchscreen panel, offering intuitive control over temperature, color, and flame speed.

This electric fireplace is ideal for small to medium-sized rooms, delivering effective heating up to 400 sq. ft. Its simple design and advanced features make it an excellent choice for those seeking a modern, eco-friendly heating solution.
