Efficient, Portable, and Multi-Functional Kerosene Heater

This SHWE kerosene heater is engineered to deliver powerful, consistent heat with an advanced metal chimney designed to enhance combustion efficiency. As a result, the paraffin burns more completely, warming your space faster and more effectively. Featuring a high output of 9000 BTU/H (equivalent to 3000 W), it's ideal for heating rooms up to 20 square meters, making it a great choice for both home and cabin use.

The fuel-efficient design consumes only 0.25 liters per hour, helping you save on fuel costs while keeping your indoor air cleaner and more comfortable. Its lightweight, durable stainless steel construction ensures long-term reliability and makes it easy to move around as needed. Perfect for those looking for a versatile solution, this heater can be used for heating, boiling water, cooking, and even grilling.

Whether you're staying indoors during a power outage or exploring outdoor spaces with no electricity, this portable oil heater offers unmatched convenience and performance. It's a smart investment for anyone who needs dependable heat and cooking capabilities in one compact, efficient device.
